Your Voice Matters — Share Your Concerns & Real-Life Stories

Community members are urged to send emails and letters (Public Comment) regarding their concerns and real-life stories about high-volume permits being allowed on this corridor and across the regional area before a traffic study and mitigation can be solved to handle the volume. Your input helps show the real-world impact of approving high-intensity uses without first requiring a Traffic Impact Analysis and the infrastructure improvements needed to support them.

Commenters are also encouraged to address concerns of procedural, substantive, and legal error: by the Zoning Administrator, who prematurely approved the land-use permit, and by the Board of County Commissioners, who voted to approve the permit while dismissing valid community safety issues.
